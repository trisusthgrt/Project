Status: Not adopted

Description

The sweet-faced, lovable Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dog breed. Labs are friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companions who have more than enough affection to go around
 for a family looking for a medium-to-large dog. Labrador Retrievers are registered in three colours: black (a solid black colour), yellow (considered from creamy white to fox-red), and
 chocolate (medium to dark brown and originally called “Liver”). The sturdy, well-balanced Labrador Retriever can, depending on the sex, stand from 21.5 to 24.5 inches at the shoulder and 
 weigh between 25 to 35 kilograms.

Life span: 10 to 12 years
