                                                                                    HYACINTH MACAW
Status: Not adopted

Description:

Hyacinth macaws are one of the largest species of parrot. They are beautiful, smart, and can even mimic human speech. They have a blue body of feathers, a solid black beak, and 
yellow circling their eyes and the lower part of their beak. They nest in pre-existing holes in trees with a clutch of two to three eggs.

Life span :50 years

Age :17 years
